New Horizon Skating Club

New Horizon skating Club is skating club based in Nairobi Kneya. Our mission is to Inspire a group of young talents to embrace and earn from the sport. We have school based programs as well as weekend teams and private lessons. For the Weekend trainings we are based at Gateway Mall Syokimau Rooftop. We offere trainings to kids as young as 3 to anyone brave enough to stand on wheels. Our classes are divided into beginners , Intermediate and professional training.
